Vital Signs Monitor

Heart Rate

-- bpm

Measures the number of heartbeats per minute.
Normal range: 60–100 bpm.

Galvanic Skin Response (GSR)

-- µS

Indicates skin conductance affected by stress and stimulation.
Normal range: 1–20 µS at rest.

Respiratory Rate

-- breaths/min

Measures the number of breaths per minute.
Normal range: 12–20 breaths/min.
